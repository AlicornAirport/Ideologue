IDEOLOGUE







Another political affiliation quiz?


Well, kind of. Based off of 8values, Ideologue is a semi-comprehensive tool to help you better find sociopolitical ideologies that align with your values.


Political quizzes (especially axis quizzes) can be a little reductive, so this isn't meant to fully describe everything you believe in, just to help you better put your beliefs into words, and maybe help you find more like-minded people. Please answer honestly!


Unlike most of these quizzes, we're not telling you what the axes are just yet, to preserve as much accuracy as we can.
